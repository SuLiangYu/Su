Multilingual : CH, EN

Project Brief

I created a series of brand images for the startup company, and the official website is the main material to promote the company's philosophy and image. The main goal of this project is to convey professional and high-tech brand image. Also provides information for promoting high tech services, which can easily be navigated by potential clients and job seekers.

Challenges

1. Build Brand Image
2. Provide Information of INC
3. Communicate Company Culture
4. Provide Contact Information

Persona

User Journey Map

Competitive Analysis

I choose three companies which are all focus on high-tech development to compare the user experience of each competitor's website.

Results

1. Most of technology companies are tend to use blue as the main represent color.
2. Provide more methods for users to get information about the company.
3. The homepage plays an important part when making first impressions for users.
4. Appropriate interactions on browsing websites can attract users' eyes.
5. Navigation is better when it's sufficient and clear.

Information Architecture

Put the most important and newest information at the home page for users to navigate them directly.

Design Features

Feature 1

High Tech, Future Sense, Simplicity

Establish the unique style of INC. At the home page, I used parallax, color gradient with fade in animations to provide users to experience the infomation interaction and convey novel impression.

Feature 2

Provide Clear Information

I want users to be able to navigate in the most efficient manner. I put the main services and newest information at the homepage. And provide buttons with an anchor feature that allows users to quickly direct themselves to the specific areas of the website.

Feature 3

Vibrant Interaction

As INC is filled with young employees, I provided interactions to indicate youthfulness.

Feature 4

Let's Get In Touch

Provided various ways that users can contact us directly. This includes online messaging feature and social media links.

Feature 5

We Want You!

On the job description page, a stakeholder had requested to add the link to another job seeker website initially. I had suggested to put a button which lets job seekers to send emails directly to our company's mailbox.
