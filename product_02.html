Material Reuse Design Project

Shell Island

Product Designer : Liang-yu Su, Yen-yu Chang

The shell's cross section and the blue resin look like the islands on the sea, just like the unique beauty of Taiwan, and we hope that through these boxes, it can remind users to pay more attention to our environment.

Project Objectives

The piles of discarded oysters by the seashore are unsightly and smelly in Taiwan. We chose to use these discarded oyster shells as materials to see whether we can find another possibility for them.



Material Analysis

Look for the possibilities of shells.

( burning / corrosion )



Process

a. Choose some shells and cut it in suitable shapes by machines

b. Put shells into resin and stir with pigments.

c. Wait until resin becomes solid and cut it in the shape we want by machines.



d. Use sander to make it smooth and cut a ditch in order to suit the metal box below.

e. Spray gloss paint to protect surface.

f. Cooperate with the factory to manufacture the metal boxes.
